JobTrackr

JobTracker is a handy app that helps you manage your job applications effectively. It allows you to create application profiles and track application statuses. With search and filtering options, JobTracker keeps you organized and on top of your job search, increasing your chances of success.

Stack: React NodeJs Mongoose and ExpressJS.
